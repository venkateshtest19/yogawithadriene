Quick Answers

Are the yoga videos really free?

Yes! All yoga videos on our YouTube channel are completely free. We believe yoga should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need equipment?

Just a yoga mat (or towel) and comfortable clothing. Some practices use props like blocks or blankets, but they're optional.

I'm a complete beginner. Can I still practice?

Absolutely! All our practices offer modifications for every level. Start with our "Yoga for Beginners" series.

How do I support the channel?

Subscribe on YouTube, share videos with friends, and join our community. Your support means everything!
