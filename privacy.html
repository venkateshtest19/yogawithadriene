Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

Yoga With Adriene is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily contact us via email. When you visit our website or watch our videos on YouTube, basic analytics may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve your experience.

2. How We Use Information

Any information you share via email is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, trade, or share your contact details with third parties. Our primary goal is to provide free, accessible yoga content to the global community.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance functionality and user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. YouTube and Google services may use cookies for measurement and advertising purposes. Please review YouTube's and Google's privacy policies for more information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website contains links to YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and other third-party platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise children's online activities.

6. YouTube Data

When you watch our videos on YouTube, YouTube may collect data according to their privacy policy. This includes viewing history, device information, and interaction data. We do not have access to personally identifiable information about individual viewers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
